Glenfiddich 12 Year Old

Origin: Scotland
Glenfiddich 12 Year Old is the world’s most awarded single malt Scotch. With notes of fresh pear, subtle oak, and a creamy finish, it’s a whisky that’s both approachable and refined. Perfect for those new to Scotch or seasoned fans!

Signature Serve: Glenfiddich Neat

  • 50ml Glenfiddich 12 Year Old
  • Optional: a splash of water

Instructions: Pour Glenfiddich into a whisky glass. Add a splash of water to open up the flavors, or enjoy neat to savor its full character.
