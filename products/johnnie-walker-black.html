Johnnie Walker Black Label

Origin: Scotland
Johnnie Walker Black Label is a world-renowned blend, aged for 12 years to perfection. Its deep, complex flavors of rich fruits, vanilla, and smoky oak make every sip a celebration. Elevate your moments with this iconic whisky!

Signature Serve: Black Label Highball

  • 50ml Johnnie Walker Black Label
  • 100ml soda water or ginger ale
  • Lemon wedge
  • Ice cubes

Instructions: Fill a tall glass with ice, pour in Black Label, top with soda or ginger ale, and garnish with a lemon wedge. Refreshing and sophisticated!
