Jameson Irish Whiskey

Origin: Ireland
Jameson Irish Whiskey is triple-distilled for exceptional smoothness and balance. Its gentle notes of vanilla, spice, and sweet sherry make it a favorite for sipping or mixing. Taste the spirit of Ireland in every glass!

Signature Serve: Jameson Ginger & Lime

  • 50ml Jameson Irish Whiskey
  • 100ml ginger ale
  • Lime wedge
  • Ice cubes

Instructions: Fill a glass with ice, add Jameson, top with ginger ale, and garnish with a lime wedge. Light, lively, and delicious!
